Mustafa Yilmaz other known self-named Can, considers himself as a generalist or a multidisciplinary artist in visual art.


Can creates digital products and experiences with passion and love - yes he can. Aiming for cinema career, he is eager to gain some professional experiences in post production. Despite, his works limited by his personal stuffs and some freelance customers, you may say he is self thought art-director. A nerd? Or a geek? Idk. He likes to integrate his work with programming and AI. And yes, he hates repetitive jobs so he always seek for practical way of doing things.
